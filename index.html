About Data Breach Detector

A data breach is a security violation, in which sensitive, protected or confidential data is copied, transmitted, viewed, stolen or used by an individual unauthorized to do so. Data breaches may involve financial information such as credit card and debit card details, bank details, personal information like email address and passwords. Incidents range from concerted attacks by individuals who hack for personal gain or malice.

Solution

We are working to create a web portal where users can come and enter their email id which is associated with various social accounts on different websites or social platforms. With a single click our site will provide a list of names of various services which have undergone data breaches with the help of third party APIs. The user can immediately take appropriate action, such as changing their passwords and other personal credentials. This will ensure that their account becomes secured again and they will be aware of such incidents.
